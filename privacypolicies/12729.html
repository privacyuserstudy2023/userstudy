Privacy

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

We do collect the usage history (like what questions were asked and what answers were given), but they are not associated with specific users. They are used in summarized form to improve the game's knowledge of animals and other similar purposes. We may share summarized statistics of the game usage, for example "Cat is the most commonly used animal".
