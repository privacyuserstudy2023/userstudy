ecobee Privacy Policy

At ecobee, we want to transform lives. But to do so, we need to have your trust. You have invited us into your home. To earn and keep that trust, we promise the following:

  • Your Personal Information and the data created by your ecobee Devices belong to you. You have the right to have a copy your Personal Information and Device Data at any time, and to be forgotten if you so choose. For instructions on how to delete or get a copy of your Personal Information and Device Data, follow these instructions.

  • If we collect it, we will safeguard it. We look after your Personal Information and Device Data like we would our own. This extends through the lifecycle of your data. Personal Information is only sent or stored on third-party systems when they abide by these principles.

  • We use only the Personal Information and Device data we need, and only when we need it. If we don’t have it, we can’t lose or mishandle it. We will collect only what is needed to deliver our services. We have strict policies and technical measures to prevent unauthorized employee access to your Personal Information and Device Data (including sound and video recordings). When Personal Information or Device Data is no longer needed for a particular purpose, we will remove or delete it.

  • We do not and will not sell your Personal Information.

We will always offer you transparency and choice. This Privacy Policy is intended to describe how your Personal Information and Device Data is processed and used, and we will make every effort to ensure that our activities keep within the spirit of this Privacy Policy, and that we fully share this information with you. Please note that by visiting and using ecobee.com, the ecobee mobile app or ecobee Devices or online service operated by ecobee, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

ecobee collects Personal Information and Device Data in order to provide, customize and improve our services. Specifically, ecobee collects Personal Information and Device data as follows:

1. ecobee Website and Store Visitors

Every computer connected to the Internet is provided with a domain name (e.g. someispname.net) and an IP Address (e.g. 192.164.13.145). When a visitor requests a page from the ecobee website, our Web Servers automatically identify and log the HTTP request that is made to our Web Server. By itself, this information reveals nothing personal about you. In fact, the only information that we automatically gather and log is as follows:

  • The IP Address of the Site that may have Referred you
  • Your IP Address
  • The Web Page that you may have linked to us from, if any
  • The Product Identifier for Version and Make of Browser (e.g. Internet Explorer®)
  • The Operating System platform that you may be running (e.g. Macintosh® or Windows®)
  • Search Words or Terms that are passed from a Search Engine (e.g. Google® or Yahoo!®)
  • Information readily available and stored in your Adobe® Flash ‘System.Capabilities’ Object.

There is absolutely nothing special about the information just described, and the practice of collecting this data has been generally standardized by virtually every Web Server on the Internet today.

The purpose for this information is actually two-fold. One, ecobee has found it advantageous to examine visitor traffic in its aggregate, so that we can ensure maximum compatibility for the various Browsers and Operating Systems that visit our site. Additionally, as any business should attest, it is our desire to generate greater amounts of visitor traffic. By analyzing visitation patterns, referring URLs and Search Engine terms, we can strategically enhance our exposure on the Internet.

Cookies are small pieces of data that are transferred to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser from our Web Server. A cookie cannot read data from your hard disk or read cookie files that may have been created from other sites. The ecobee website utilizes cookies only as a means of providing personalization features to our visitors. For example, we utilize cookies to manage your login to your personalized web portal once you have registered your ecobee Device. In addition, ecobee may use cookies to assist users in completing an incomplete purchase for items left in their shopping cart.

You can choose whether to accept cookies by changing the settings of your browser. Typically, by accessing the browser’s help feature you can obtain information on how to prevent your browser from accepting all cookies or to notify you when a cookie is being sent. If you choose not to accept these cookies, your experience at our website and other websites may be diminished and some features may not work as intended.

Our Site may contain links to other websites operated by third parties such as media sites and app stores and may include social media features such as Facebook and Twitter buttons (such as “Like,” “Tweet” or “Pin”). These third-party sites may collect information about you if you click on a link and the social media sites may automatically record information about your browsing behavior every time you visit a site that has a social media button. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing the feature, not by our privacy policy. We do not control what information these third parties collect. Please review your privacy settings on your social media sites and think carefully before clicking on links which may take you to a third-party website.

In addition, we permit third-party partners to use cookies and other technology to collect information about your browsing activities over time and across different websites when you use our Site. This cookie information may be used to generate custom and lookalike audiences and similar marketing outreaches.

Our Site does not knowingly collect or store any Personal Information about children under the age of 13.

3. ecobee Device Users

If you register your Device (e.g., an ecobee SmartThermostat, SmartCamera or Smartswitch) with ecobee.com and create an ecobee account, you will be required to provide an e-mail address to associate with your ecobee account (“ecobee Account Data”). When you have registered your Device and created an ecobee account, you will be able to remotely view and control your Device from anywhere on the internet via a mobile application or our web portal.

Your ecobee Device will collect environmental data such as temperature and humidity as well as operational data such as temperature set points from your HVAC equipment. Some Device models may include additional types of data such motion sensing (i.e., “occupancy sensing”). Depending on your Device model, your Device may also collect data from remote sensors in addition to the Device itself. Using machine learning with both on-device and remote processing, environmental, operational HVAC and occupancy sensing data and is used to optimize the heating and cooling algorithms on your Device to minimize energy usage when you are home or away. Collectively, this is known as Device Data. For ecobee Smart Security users, occupancy-sensing data can be used to arm or disarm your home monitoring solution.

To enable these remote functions, your Device needs to transmit Device Data to the web portal. These transmissions are fully encrypted. The Device Data will be stored by the web portal to provide you with historical reports. When you install your ecobee Device, you’ll be asked for Personal Information like your name, home address, postal/ZIP code as well home details (like house age, number of floors, etc.), which is considered ecobee Account Data. We use both Device Data and ecobee Account Data to make more intelligent decisions about how to manage your heating and cooling, and to reduce your energy bills. We may also anonymously aggregate your Device Data with Device Data from other homes in order to create reports, benchmarks and allow you (and others) to compare energy usage, on an anonymous basis, with similar homes. ecobee may also send you information and reports regarding your Device’s operations, software updates, energy-savings opportunities or promotions directly onscreen in the web portal, or via email.

By registering your Device, you agree to receive e-mails from ecobee relating to software updates for your Device, mobile apps and web portal, as well as status updates for web portal service. You can opt out of receiving these update emails via the “Unsubscribe” link at the bottom of every update e-mail. You also agree to receive e-mailed HVAC alerts and reminders, and to provide a contact email for customer support. You can disable e-mailed HVAC alerts and reminders using the ecobee web portal.

From time to time, ecobee may send you energy savings advice, as well as marketing materials related to ecobee and related third-party product offerings. You can opt out of receiving these update emails via the “Unsubscribe” link at the bottom of every update e-mail. Ecobee will not give your e-mail address to a third-party without your consent.

You can delete your ecobee account and associated ecobee Account Data, as well as Device Data by deregistering your Device(s) using the web portal or directly on the Device.

5. eco+ and Utility Programs

eco+ works by monitoring your energy use, the usage rate structure of your utility provider and any Demand Response programs and Demand Response events that your Utility or its third party partners and implementors of such programs may issue (individually or collectively, “Utility”). In order to operate and make eco+ available, ecobee collects, and may disclose, certain information from you with and from your Utility. ecobee Account Data and Device Data collected or shared by ecobee with your Utility (including its program implementors) or, to the extent required by law, the relevant Regulatory body, may include: your Name, your Address, Housing characteristics, your utility account number (if provided by you), your thermostat serial number, your thermostat data and your HVAC usage data. Personal Information collected or shared by your Utility with ecobee may include: your Name, your Address, your Utility account number, Utility account information, your utility meter ID, and utility meter data (collectively, “shared data”). Subject to any applicable statutes, regulations or regulatory decisions or policies, shared data may be used for the purposes of assessing your eligibility to participate in Utility Programs, verification of your participation in Utility Programs, making intelligent decisions relating to energy production and conservation, providing Demand Response and Energy Measurement and verification services to a Utility or its governing regulatory body. ecobee may also use the shared data to improve its services, including eco+.

If you agree to participate in an ecobee eco+ or utility-sponsored electricity management, usage or Demand Response program (“Utility Program”), ecobee, your Utility as well as any utility system operators (collectively, “program implementers”) may share account information to the extent necessary to validate your eligibility and participation in the Utility Program. In order to implement the Utility program, the program implementors may need to associate your accounts held between the different program partners.

The program implementors may also use your ecobee Account Data, Device Data and electricity use in order to make intelligent decisions relating to electricity production and conservation, and to provide Demand Response and Energy Measurement and Verification reports and services. Data contained in these EM&V reports and services may be shared with the appropriate utility system implementors and regulators as part of the Utility Program. ecobee may also use this data to improve its services, including the Utility Program. The program implementer(s) may also anonymously aggregate your data with data from other homes in order to create reports, benchmarks and allow you (and others) to compare energy usage, on an anonymous basis, with similar homes.

Your Utility and program implementer will not sell, rent, or otherwise give your ecobee Account Data or Device Data to a third-party for purposes unrelated to the Utility Program, without your consent.

9. Voice Services

Certain ecobee Devices are Alexa Enabled Products, meaning they are capable of being voice-controlled using the Alexa Voice Service (AVS) provided by Amazon Digital Services LLC (“Amazon”). When AVS is enabled on your ecobee Device, your Device continuously listens for “wake words”. When the ecobee Device hears one of these wake words, it will initiate a brief audio recording and transmit that recorded message to Amazon for voice recognition processing. Use of AVS and third party services via AVS are governed by the Alexa Terms of Use (as provided on the Amazon.com website). Any information you provide to Amazon will be treated in accordance with the Amazon Privacy Notice (also available on the Amazon.com website), not the ecobee Privacy Policy. ecobee does not have direct access to, or retain any copies of, your recorded voice messages to AVS. When your Alexa-enabled ecobee Device is in Private mode, it is not listening for wake words, and no recorded messages are sent to Amazon.

10. Video Services

ecobee SmartCameras can stream and store recorded audio and video in the cloud. No one can see your video without your permission or a court order. Video is processed directly on your SmartCamera and recordings and snapshots are stored securely on the cloud for 14 days. All recordings and snapshots that are saved to the cloud are visible in the ecobee mobile app, so you know exactly what clips have been sent and saved. When you delete a video from the ecobee app it will also erase that video from the cloud. For ecobee Smart Security customers, your SmartCamera can also be used to help determine occupancy and when to enable or disable your home monitoring system.
