Protecting Your Rights

As a data subject you have rights over your personal data, we think it’s important that these rights are protected. These are a simple summary of your data subject rights, and are not exhaustive, more precise information on exactly what these data subject rights entail can be found on the website of the Information Commissioner's Office.

  • Your right of access and portability. You have the right to know what personal data we collect, and what happens to it. This is described in this policy.
  • Your right to be informed. You have the right to a copy of your personal data, including how and why we are using your personal data. Copies of data we provide will be in a structured and machine-readable format.
  • Your right to rectification. If any of the personal data we use is incorrect or inaccurate, you have the right for it to be rectified.
  • Your right to erasure. Also known as the right to be forgotten. If you are no longer using our services, including if you uninstall our service your data will be erased.
  • Your right to restrict processing. If you do not want your personal data used for a specific reason, you have the right to ask that we stop.
  • Your right to object. You have the right to object to the way we use your personal data, and the absolute right to opt out of any direct marketing. We do not store or use any of your personal data for the purpose of direct marketing.
  • Automated decision making and profiling. We do not use automated decision making and profiling in our site or services.

To exercise any of your rights over your personal data please send your request to privacy@digitaldesignlabs.com and we will aim to complete any request within 28 days. If you are unhappy with the way in which we have handled your request, you have the right to make a complaint to the ICO. These rights are not absolute, and any requests made to us relating to your personal data rights are still subject to legal requirements, including gambling regulations and reporting obligations.
